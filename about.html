Introduction

I'm a fresh graduate with good foundation in Web Development, RDBMS, and UI/UX Design.

I am pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at the Polytechnic University of the Philippines - Sto. Tomas.

I initially chose this degree program for practical reasons, setting aside my passion for Science, specifically Biology. But to my surprise, I found myself enjoying this field even more than I had anticipated.

I started on this degree with no prior programming knowledge. It wasn't until my second year that I discovered a genuine interest in the field. Through diligent efforts and dedication, I consistently achieved the President's List award every semester from 2019 to 2023, maintaining a prominent position within the class. As a result of my hard work, I graduated magna cum laude with the Class of 2023.
